{% docs extra_type %}

| Type of Extra    | Definition                                                                                      |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **No Ball**      | Runs awarded to the batting team when the bowler delivers an illegal ball (e.g., overstepping). |
| **Wide Ball**    | Runs awarded when the bowler delivers the ball out of the batter's reach outside the wide line. |
| **Bye**          | Runs taken when the ball passes the batter without touching their bat or body.                  |
| **Leg Bye**      | Runs taken when the ball hits the batter's body (not the bat) and no attempt to play a shot.    |
| **Penalty Runs** | Runs awarded to a team for breaches of the rules by the opposing team (e.g., unfair play).      |
